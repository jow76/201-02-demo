Site Accessibility

Part of my course's lab is to check this website's accessibility rating by running it through Lighthouse on Google Chrome, then post the results here. I'm happy with a score of 91 for my personal site but understand why it is not 100. If I were making the site for a business, I would make the text easier to read against the background and will now look into the head of each page and order the elements properly.
